Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

AI Doomsday Toolbox is designed with privacy first. All AI processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

1. Data Collection

AI Doomsday Toolbox does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and locally on your device.

  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking services
  • No telemetry: No usage data is sent to us or third parties
  • No accounts: The app does not require user registration

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • AI Models: Downloaded models for chat, image generation, and transcription
  • User Preferences: Settings and configuration options
  • Generated Content: Images, transcriptions, and videos you create
  • Notes: Notes you create within the app
  • Chat History: Conversations with AI models

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To load AI models from your storage and save generated content. We use MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE to allow AI models (which can be several GB) to be stored in user-preferred locations without duplication.
  • Microphone: For audio recording in the transcription feature (processed locally)
  • Camera: For QR code scanning to connect devices on local network
  • Internet: Required only for downloading AI models from HuggingFace and for optional local network features
  • Notifications: To show progress during AI processing tasks

4. Network Features

The app includes optional network features that operate on your local network only:

  • Model Sharing: Share AI models with other devices on your WiFi
  • File Server: Access files from other devices on your network
  • LLM Server: Use the AI chat from other apps on your network
  • Kiwix Server: Serve offline Wikipedia to other devices

5. Third-Party Services

The app may connect to:

  • HuggingFace: For browsing and downloading AI models (optional, user-initiated)
  • Kiwix Catalog: For browsing available offline Wikipedia packages (optional)

No personal data is shared with these services.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
